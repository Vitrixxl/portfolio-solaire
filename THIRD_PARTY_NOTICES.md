# Third-party visual assets

## NASA planet models

The GLB files in `public/models/planets` are official NASA 3D resources. The
models are credited to NASA Visualization Technology Applications and
Development (VTAD), except for Mars, credited to NASA/JPL-Caltech. Their
embedded PNG textures were converted to WebP for web delivery; the model
geometry is otherwise preserved.

Source pages:

- [Mercury](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/mercury-3d-model/)
- [Venus](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/venus-3d-model/)
- [Earth](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/earth-3d-model/)
- [Mars](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/planet-mars-3d-model/)
- [Jupiter](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/jupiter-3d-model/)
- [Saturn](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/saturn-3d-model/)
- [Uranus](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/uranus-3d-model/)
- [Neptune](https://science.nasa.gov/resource/neptune-3d-model/)

Use of these assets is subject to the
[NASA Images and Media Usage Guidelines](https://www.nasa.gov/nasa-brand-center/images-and-media/).

## Supplemental textures

The Earth cloud and Moon textures in `public/textures/planets` come from the
[Solar System Scope Texture Library](https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/),
created by Solar System Scope and distributed under the
[Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
The copies were obtained from the
[Solar System Scope collection on Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Solar_System_Scope).
